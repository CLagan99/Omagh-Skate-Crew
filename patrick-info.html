Who is Patrick?

Patrick - OSC Member


Patrick is a past member of Omagh Skate Crew.

Patrick started skateboarding back in 2016 and has shown his skills on his YouTube channel, Paddy Skates15.

Patrick sadly had very little time to skate with other members of OSC but has made a huge contribution to the skateboarding scene of Omagh.
