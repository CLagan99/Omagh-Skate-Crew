Who is Darren?

Oliver - OSC Member


Darren is a one of the core members of Omagh Skate Crew.

Darren had a passion for skateboarding from a young age and has progressed over his skills and abilities over the years.

Since he became married and is now a father, Darren has continued to promote skateboarding within the Omagh and surrounding areas for company 'Urban Motion'.
