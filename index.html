Welcome to Omagh Skate Crew


Welcome to the official Omagh Skate Crew website. Here, you can find the latest information about OSC, see its upcoming events and get to know our past and current members of the crew.


Omagh Skate Crew aims to make a huge contribution to the skateboarding community within the Omagh and surrounding areas. This has been a huge success through the documentation of skateboarding clips from the members to present them on their social media platforms, such as YouTube, Instagram and Facebook.


Explore your way through the website to know what OSC means to the community of Omagh, have an insight of every member within the crew, and many more.
