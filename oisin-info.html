Who is Oisin?

Oisin - OSC Member


Oisin is a one of the core and past members of Omagh Skate Crew.

Oisin had a passion for skateboarding back in 2016 where he met fellow members Oliver, Anton and Ciaran, and has documented his skills on his YouTube channel, Omagh Rollers.

Sadly, Oisin has left the skateboarding scene due to sport and work commitments but his contribution will last forever, as well as the craic.
