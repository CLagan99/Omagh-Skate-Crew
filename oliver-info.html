Who is Oliver?

Oliver - OSC Member


Oliver is a one of the core and past members of Omagh Skate Crew.

Oliver had a passion for skateboarding from a young age and has progressed his skills and abilities over the years.

Now a married man, Oliver sadly has very little time to skate with other members of OSC but is a true legend in the art of skateboarding in Omagh.
