Who is Ciaran?

Ciaran - OSC Member


Ciaran is a one of the core members of Omagh Skate Crew.

Orignailly from Derry, Ciaran had a passion for skateboarding ever since he was 9 years old.

Ciaran has documented his skateboarding videos on his YouTube channel, Ciaran1289, with friends and fellow members such as Anton, Oliver, Darren and so many more.
