Who is Deon?

Deon - OSC Member


Deon is a past member of Omagh Skate Crew.

Similar to OSC Member Patrick, Deon started skateboarding back in 2016 where he has shown his skills and abilities.

Deon sadly had very little time to skate with other members of OSC but has made a huge contribution to the skateboarding scene of Omagh.
